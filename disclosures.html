Step 2: Escalation to Regulators

If your complaint is not redressed within 30 days, or if you are unsatisfied with the resolution provided, you may escalate the matter directly to the respective regulators:

  • Mutual Funds Grievance: You can file a complaint with AMFI online through their portal at www.amfiindia.com or use the SEBI SCORES platform.
  • Insurance Grievance: You can escalate the complaint to the Integrated Grievance Management System (IGMS) of IRDAI at www.irdai.gov.in or call the IRDAI Grievance Call Centre at 155255.
